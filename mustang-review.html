Mustang GTD

The Mustang GTD Just Smashed the Nürburgring…ish

So, the Ford Mustang GTD just dropped a ~6:40 at the Nürburgring—and yeah, that's insanely fast. Like, "there's definitely something a bit fishy here…" fast. But before we crown it king of the Green Hell, let's talk about the fine print.

Speed With a Side of "Optimized"

This wasn't your average showroom-spec run. Rumor mill says:

  • Tyres were closer to track monsters than road rubber
  • Setup was… let's call it enthusiast plus-plus mode
  • Not exactly what you'll get with the keys and a warranty

So, comparing it to something like a Porsche 911 GT3 RS? Bit like bringing racing shoes to a barefoot race.

Still—Let's Not Downplay It

A 6:40 lap isn't luck. That means serious aero, incredible grip, and serious intent. This isn't muscle car territory anymore. This is track weapon cosplay turning into reality.

The Verdict

Messy record? Maybe. Impressive? Definitely. Now we just wait for Ford to do it again… this time without the asterisks.
